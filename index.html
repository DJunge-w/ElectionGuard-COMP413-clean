Introduction

The election of the mayor and the treasurer of the City of New County is an on-line election in which citizens of the city who are registered to vote can cast ballots to the candidates. For a valid voting, you will have to select one mayor, while the selection of the treasurer is optional.

Instruction

Mark the oval to the bottom of the name of your choice. Review before you submit. In the end, you will receive two QR codes and tracking numbers of your vote.
